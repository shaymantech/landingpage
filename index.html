Admission is Open for 2023/2024

It is yet another year of opportunities as the doors of Greatprints International School are now open for the admission of students into JSS 1, JSS 2 and JSS 3 for the 2023/2024 session. We are immensely grateful to God and all those who have surported our vision and mission since we started this great school.
The entrance examination dates for the 2023/2024 academic session are as follows:

  • 1st April, 2023
  • 13th May, 2023 and
  • 5th August 2023

    • The entrance examination can be written at either of the following centers:

  • Greatprints International School, Owo, Ondo State.
  • Fingerprints Hotels and Suites, Oba-Ile, Akure, Ondo State.
  • Time:10a.m. prompt(for each of the dates)
    • Provision has also been made for students who will prefer a virtual entrance examination.

    Also important is the fact that scholarships will be awarded to the three most outstanding students for the first term of the next academic session. A full schorlarship will be awarded to the most outstanding students while a 100% and 50% tuition-free schorlarship awarded to the second and third most outstanding students respectively.

    Admission forms which sell for seven thousand five hundred naira only (#7,500) can be purchased at either Greatprints International School, Owo or Fingerprints Hotels and suites (Owo & Akure).
